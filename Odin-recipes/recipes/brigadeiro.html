Brigadeiro

Description

Brigadeiro is an iconic Brazilian dessert, a must-have at birthday parties and celebrations. It's a creamy chocolate fudge, traditionally rolled into small balls and covered in sprinkles. Its simplicity to make and unmistakable flavor make it a national favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk (395g)
  • 4 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles for coating
  • Butter for greasing hands

Instructions

  1. In a thick-bottomed pan, combine the condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter.
  2. Cook over low heat, stirring continuously with a spatula to prevent it from sticking to the bottom.
  3. Cook for about 10 to 15 minutes, or until the mixture starts to pull away from the bottom of the pan (the rolling point).
  4. Pour the mixture onto a greased plate and let it cool completely.
  5. With buttered hands, take small portions of the mixture and roll them into balls.
  6. Roll the balls in chocolate sprinkles until they are completely covered.
  7. Place the brigadeiros in paper cups and serve. Enjoy!
