Chicken Medallions with Bacon

Description

Chicken medallions with bacon is a juicy and flavorful dish, perfect for a special meal. The combination of tender chicken with the crispiness and smoky flavor of bacon is simply irresistible. It's easy to prepare and is sure to impress anyone.

Ingredients

  • 500g of chicken breast fillets
  • 150g of bacon slices (1 slice per medallion)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced (optional)
  • Olive oil for grilling
  • Toothpicks to secure the bacon

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken breasts into thick pieces (about 3-4 cm thick), in the shape of medallions.
  2. Season the chicken medallions on all sides with salt, black pepper, and the minced garlic.
  3. Wrap a slice of bacon around each medallion and secure the end with a toothpick so it doesn't come loose.
  4. Heat a drizzle of olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Place the medallions in the skillet and sear all sides, starting with the edges to brown the bacon.
  6. Once they are golden brown on the outside, reduce the heat, cover the skillet, and let it cook for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Remove the toothpicks before serving. It goes great with rice and a salad.
